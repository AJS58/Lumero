HEIC / HEIF

HEIC is efficient and modern, but can be less compatible with older Windows PCs, legacy editing software and some upload systems. Lumero should not mark it as bad — only as a workflow consideration.

Windows Friendly Mode

For maximum compatibility: Settings › Camera › Formats › Most Compatible.

Recommended actions

Enable Optimised Battery Charging

Your iPhone can learn your daily charging routine and delay charging past 80% until you need it. This helps reduce time spent at 100%, which can slow battery ageing.

Go to: Settings › Battery › Charging.

Important
Avoid unnecessary heat

Heat accelerates battery ageing. Remove thick cases if the phone gets warm while charging.
